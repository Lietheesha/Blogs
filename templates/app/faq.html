FAQ

How can I reset my password?

You can reset your password by clicking the 'Change Password' link on the login page and following the instructions sent to your email address.

How do I update my email address?

Log in to your account, navigate to the 'Profile Settings' section, and update your email address there. A confirmation link will be sent to the new address.

How do I create a new blog post?

To create a new blog post, click on the 'New Post' button in the navigation bar, fill out the form with your blog content, and submit it.
